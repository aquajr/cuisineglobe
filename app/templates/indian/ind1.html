RECIPE 1

Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix chicken with yogurt, salt, and 1 teaspoon each of turmeric and red chili powder. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, melt 1 tablespoon of butter and cook the marinated chicken until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt the remaining butter.
  4. Saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  7. Add the remaining turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add the cooked chicken back to the pan.
  9. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  11. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
