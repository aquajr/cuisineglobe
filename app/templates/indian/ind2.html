RECIPE 2

Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 grams chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)
  • 4 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Fried onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse 2 cups of basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. Boil the rice in salted water until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. Mix 500 grams of chicken with 1 cup yogurt, 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, and salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Heat 4 tablespoons oil or ghee in a pan. Add the sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  7. Add the marinated chicken and cook until fully done.
  8. Add 2 slit green chilies, 1 teaspoon garam masala, and the chopped coriander and mint leaves. Mix well.
  9. In a pot, spread a layer of the partially cooked rice. Add a layer of the chicken mixture. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of rice on top.
  10. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low flame for 20-25 minutes.
  11. Garnish with fried onions. Serve hot with raita or salad.
