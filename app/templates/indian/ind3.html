RECIPE 3

Tandoori Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, oil, garlic, ginger, cumin, turmeric, paprika, garam masala, chili powder, and salt to make a marinade.
  2. Marinate the chicken in the mixture for at least 4 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  4. Bake the chicken for 25-30 minutes until cooked and slightly charred.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve.
